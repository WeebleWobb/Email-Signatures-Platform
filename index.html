Email Signatures

  • For Mac
  • For PC

Follow Directions

  1. Fill out the form for the appropriate company and press submit.
  2. When email signature appears on screen press, CMD - A to select all, then CMD - C to copy.
  3. In outlook, click Outlook on the top menu bar, then click Preferences.
  4. Under Email, click Signatures.
  5. Under Signature name, click Add a signature '+'.
  6. Double-click Untitled, and enter a name for the new signature.
  7. Click the field to place your cursor, then press CMD - V to paste copied signature into signature content area.
  8. Locate and click the Paste Options Icon and select Keep Source Formatting
  9. Under Choose default signature, select your newly created signature to set as your default signature.

Follow Directions

  1. Fill out the form for the appropriate company and press submit.
  2. When email signature appears on screen press, CTRL - A to select all, then CTRL - Cto copy.
  3. Within Outlook, select File, then select Options.
  4. Select the Mail tab, then select Signatures.
  5. Select New and type in a name for your signature.
  6. Press CTRL - V within the Edit Signature section to paste your copied signature.
  7. Under Choose default signature, select your newly created signature to set as your default signature.
