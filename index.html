Email Signatures

  • For Mac
  • For PC

Follow Directions or Watch Tutorials

  1. Fill out the corresponding form and press submit
  2. When email signature appears on screen press CMD - A to select all
  3. Locate the Outlook menu above, click Preferences
  4. Under Email, click Signatures
  5. Under Signature name, click Add a signature '+'
  6. Double-click Untitled, and enter a name for the new signature.
  7. Press CMD - V to paste copyied signature into signature content area
  8. Under Choose default signature, select your newly created signature to set as your default signature for new messages and for replies and forwards.

Follow Directions or Watch Tutorials

  1. Fill out the corresponding form and press submit
  2. When email signature appears on screen press CTRL - A to select all
  3. Press CTRL - C to copy all
  4. Within Outlook select File and then select Options
  5. Select the Mail tab and then select Signatures
  6. Select New and type in a name for your signature
  7. Press CTRL - V within the Edit Signature section to paste your copied signature
  8. Under Choose default signature, select your newly created signature to set as your default signature for new messages and for replies and forwards.
